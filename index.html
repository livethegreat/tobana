About Tobana

Access to sanitation is a necessity and a basic human right. Unfortunately, sanitation infrastructure is still inaccessible in parts of South Africa, especially in urban areas, where you would find 10 households, sharing only one mobile toilet. So in these circumstances, what about disabled people, how are they even surviving in these situations?
These are the problems that needs to be solved in South Africa, and which the government was trying for years to solve, fortunately there is a solution, Tobana is a solution.

What is Tobana?

Tobana is a portable toilet and sanitation company that offers a safe and dignified waterless sanitary for less privileged areas. The company serves disabled people.
